Your Bio

Data has shown that most men don't read a Tinder bio until after they've matched. However, most women DO read your bio.

Create a bio that, again, makes you stand out. List all the things that make you unique. Be a douchebag, show off your material belongings and accomplishments. Make someone yearn for more and want to ask questions.

That's what makes you stand out. Word it so you sound like the man from Wolf of Wall Street.

Starting a Conversation

So what now? Starting a conversation can be quite the task.

Again, you want to stand out. Don't come out with the cheesy pick-up line or 'what's up, how are you'.

Comment on something in their bio if you notice something interesting or a mutual thing you both share. Or comment on a photo if you notice a conversation starter that you can lead with.
